About EasyAspataal

Easy Aspataal is a first ever digital hospital admission desk platform in India. With a drive to change the hospitalization process in India. Mr. Manoj Gupta, Founder Easy Aspataal approached Codemischief for E2E product development. The pain point EasyAspataal is trying to solve is eliminating all or maximum human intervention in an admission process for the Hospital Ecosystem.

The Challenges

When EA founders approached Codemischief with an idea, the foremost challenge we accepted was Market analysis and understanding the existing procedures followed by the hospitals. EA fitted in the right spot with Gap analysis conducted for the Indian Healthcare System. Designing the right features with almost no baseline for reference was the most challenging part. Another catch was to get maximum product visibility in SEO using the right strategy for search engine features.

Why Codemischief

  1. Requirement analysis, Strategy planning, Technical architecture design
  2. UI/UX Design (an iterative process)
  3. Software Development.

Codemischief became a one stop shop to understand the requirement and implement the same as a workflow while fulfilling criteria of MVP. Each feature was evaluated for its feasibility and priority in every aspect and a MVP was designed.

Seamless digital on boarding of individual hospitals was designed and additional software module was developed and implemented for smooth operations.

The key features implemented were User Authentication through all channels, use of Google Places API for search engine, Cross platform integration, Integration with third party payment & e-signing services and Notification engine across all channels. The overall project management was carried out with JIRA.

The product being in data sensitive space, adhering to data security was crucial. With Codemischiefâ€™s expertise in security compliance in healthcare organizations, data security was handled with foremost priority in Architecture design.

Benefits

The customer first approach fitted right with healthcare operations and was accepted by hospitals. With HIPAA compliance for google cloud storage in place, both EasyAspataal and healthcare organizations can have confidence in data security. With a strong tech baseline Easy Aspataal has started its startup journey and is currently taken up by an accelerator and on the road to acquire funding.
