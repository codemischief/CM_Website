How important is UI/UX?

Better Customer Satisfaction equals Enhanced ROI

Unique UI/UX design offers your customers with engaging content and simple navigation. Happy and satisfied customers always spread a word for your application among their connections. A solid loyal consumer base for your product and major return buyers will lead to increase in ROI

Helps You Develop an Understanding for Your Audience

Prior to starting designing a UI/UX, it is crucial to get understanding of the needs and wants of your targeted audience. You can easily convert your prospective customers into your loyal customers and so, your sales can increase.

Builds the Brand

When you invest in an efficient UI/UX design, you give a key to satisfaction to your customers. Users will be willingly pointing out the features that can be upgraded, and thus, you invest in enhancing your application. This raises the credibility of the company as a client relationship is created with the company and product in the best way.

Codemischief’s Process for designing and prototyping UI/UX

Gaining Business insights, know your market & user needs, user and market profiling

Create a well-polished interface prototype that resonates with user and a detailed design specification

Convert prototype into a high fidelity wireframes, conduct usability testing for usability goals

Usable & scalable wireframes with framework and asset/style guide
