How Do We Do That?

We adopt a service-oriented approach to build enterprise solutions for our customers. Our expertise is in Javascript, although we can work on a gamut of technologies to deliver what it needs for an enterprise solution. You can check out our Case Studies to understand our developed solutions on the clouds and how our customers have benefited from our association. Broadly the following methodology is followed for an enterprise solution development.

UI UX Process

We Lay A Lot Of Emphasis On User Experience Design.

Achieved by conducting thorough research and intensive exploration to understand the target market, client conduct and so on, we likewise make personas for the equivalent and endeavour to ensure that the application is easy to use and intuitive simultaneously.

Our smoothed-out cycle shapes your thoughts into splendid products.

With the infiltration of digital media today, every application seems to be like the other. Our UI/UX group endeavours to ensure that each application UI web design is extraordinary and at the same time user-friendly. Client association is in each progression of the UI run, and we ensure that your contributions to the design are incorporated accurately. We also work with the team to create a comprehensive set of features that are essential to the success of our clients We create a user interface that is intuitive and user-friendly.

Development Methodology

We follow Agile methodology with strict SDLC practices to deliver a WoW product. Ours is a time-tested methodology to churn the best web applications. Our team builds web-based applications using the Javascript framework (React.js & Node.js) and any SQL or NoSQL databases. On the Big Data side, we are proficient in working on Scala, Python, and NoSQL databases. We support almost all browsers and are optimized for both Mobile and the Web world.

Requirement Analysis

Our Business Analysis team interviews the customer and documents the requirements post customer sign off into our Agile project management tool.

Planning

The Business Analysis team and development/testing team analyze the requirement and any clarification needed from the customer is taken. At this stage, the overall product development is divided into chunks that can be delivered in the agreed time duration ( 14-day plan).

Design

Our UI/UX team starts working on the product design and wireframe are shared with the customer for sign off.

Development and Testing

The 14-day plan is strictly adhered to and an assigned project manager tracks the development and testing in a given sprint. At the end of the sprint, the deliverable is deployed on a UAT environment where the customer checks and gives a sign-off. This methodology is followed until all the functionalities and the final product is delivered.

Releases

Our release cycle depends on the sprints. Our development team uses a development environment and the development release is a Q-Tag. The QA tests the Q-Tag release against test cases and once all the test cases have passed, they release with R-Tag which is deployed on UAT. We use GIT for code versioning.

Post Production Support and Maintenance

Post production and support is provided on the project. In case of maintenance a change request log is maintained in order to keep track of changes and support requests.

Hosting

We advocate and use GCP (Google Cloud Platform) for hosting our applications. Google Cloud Platform not only allows us access to one of the largest networks in the world but also ensures customers have less downtime. GCP popularity has increased tremendously in the past couple of years and we are experts in utilising Google Services. Having said that, we are equally comfortable using any other alternate cloud platforms as per customer requirements.

Application Development

We are experts in web and desktop application development, covering both front-end and backend development. We are focused on the development of high load and intricate web applications which meet business targets and automate processes.

System Architecture Design

We architect systems according to your functional and nonfunctional requirements and use state-of-the-art architecture that stands the test of time, volume of data and growing features of your application/s.

Database Design

We are experts in database design, specializing in latest trends like Mongodb, Cassandra, and Hive. Our design philosophy incorporates data integrity, data governance and efficiency.

Cloud Integration

Our engineers deploy the best and most efficient scripts and applications to leading cloud platforms like AWS and GCP, enabling smooth operations of the application/s for your business.

Quality Assurance

We ensure that your application works flawlessly across different user environments, going through a very rigorous and uncompromising testing process, thus ensuring 100% quality everytime.

Mobility Solutions

We expertly design, develop and deploy sophisticated mobility solutions, encompassing but not limited to Native Android, Native IOS, React Native.
