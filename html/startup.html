Our Services

Software development for startups

In-depth business analysis

Thorough Understanding of your business idea, MVP requirements and building a solid project specification which guide you through the product development process.

MVP Development

Full-cycle software development, front-end and back-end development, full lifecycle testing, and deployment of the system.

Scaling

Scaling your MVP with the help of additional features based on your users’ feedback.Growth hacking through customer satisfactions, retention, conversion & innovation by data driven implementation.

Empowering startups at every stage

If you wish to validate your idea, consider building an MVP first, a basic version covering the product’s fundamental components and features targeting the core requirements and needs of end-users. An MVP complies with the main principles of the lean startup methodology: build, measure, learn. Learning from initial customers is easier with an MVP than without.

If you’ve already validated features for your idea then it's time your MVP must scale. Keep your early bird customers happy with positive momentum by upgrading features your existing customers love and grow with new customers by expanding your offerings

With fast paced market growth it is important to revamp your product to stay relevant. Even recently designed products may need redesign or modernization if it does not satisfy all the user’s needs. Our tech experts will provide right guidance to identify and implement right technology to meet market

If you wish to rebuild and restructure, our rescue services are for you. Our experts will perform code review, plan a scope for improvement, add features and rebuild if needed. It is important to have a solid and well designed product architecture for an efficient product system. Our experienced software architects will provide an improved version of product architecture to give optimal performance ensuring minimal system disruptions.

OUR PROCESS

How we work with your startup Product

Discovery

1 week

First we help you to develop strategy and define goals. Work with your consulatants to find the key feature that defines your product and establish the product scope.

Agile Software Development

6 week

Having Defined the requirement, our developers start laying the foundation for the build. By the time the design is finalized, the tech team is ready to integrate it with the functionality and do its development magic. Already at this stage the product goes rigorous testing to insure a successful introduction to market.
